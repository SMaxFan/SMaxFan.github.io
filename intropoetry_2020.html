Introduction to Poetry 2020

Welcome! This course runs for eight weeks and covers a narrow set of poems. It teaches close reading and essay writing from scratch. Students tend to come from state or rural schools. If you are enrolled at an inner city/independent school, please consider whether your enrolment is in the social interest.

Papers are collected and graded on a weekly basis. The zoom link does not change week-to-week. Please do not hestitate to email me if you have questions.

I do not permit mid-cycle enrolment. In the current cycle (November/December) there are 19 students.
