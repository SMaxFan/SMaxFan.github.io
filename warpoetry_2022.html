War Poetry 2022

This is a close reading course focused on developing sharp analysis and pithy writing. It targets high achieving students of all ages. No background in close reading or poetry is necessary. I expect to enrol a class of 20, with a substantial waitlist. If you are interested, please supply this form. [This from is now closed.]

Papers are collected and graded on a weekly basis. They range from body paragraphs to full essays. We meet on Sundays at the Hawthorn Public Library conference room from 7 to 9pm.

I do not permit mid-cycle enrolment. Applications for the course are considered on a rolling basis (for the subsequent cycle). Please keep an eye on this page if you have previously applied.

Fee negotiations are discouraged. If you (the student) wish to take this course but are in financial difficulty, please write to me directly. I have reserved three places for non-fee paying students.
